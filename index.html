Bio

I'm Roslyn — a data scientist and researcher passionate about the human side of AI. I graduated from Washington State University with training spanning social psychology, neuroscience, programming, and advanced statistics.

My work explores how AI systems are built, evaluated, and deployed responsibly:

  • Evaluating sustainable limits of AI infrastructure across regions
  • Using machine learning in healthcare and agriculture
  • Studying how AI shapes human decision-making in high-stakes contexts
